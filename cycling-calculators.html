2026 Power Training Methods

🎯 Polarized Power Training

80% Easy (Zone 1-2):

  • • Zone 1: <55% FTP - Active recovery
  • • Zone 2: 55-75% FTP - Aerobic base
  • • Benefits: Fat oxidation, mitochondrial density
  • • Feel: Conversational effort, nose breathing
  • • Duration: 1-6+ hours

20% Hard (Zone 4-6):

  • • Zone 4: 90-105% FTP - Threshold
  • • Zone 5: 105-120% FTP - VO2 max
  • • Zone 6: 120-150% FTP - Anaerobic
  • • Benefits: Lactate buffering, VO2 max
  • • Duration: 3-40 minute intervals
