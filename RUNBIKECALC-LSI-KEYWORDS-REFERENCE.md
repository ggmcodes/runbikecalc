## Quick LSI Integration Formula

For each page, follow this pattern:

**Per Section (approx. 500 words)**:
- 1-2 LSI keywords in opening sentence
- 1-2 LSI keywords in middle paragraph
- 1-2 LSI keywords in closing paragraph

**Per Heading**:
- 1 LSI keyword per H2 (naturally incorporated)
- 1 LSI keyword per H3 (where applicable)

**Per Page Total**:
- 25-30 different LSI keywords distributed
- 60-80 total LSI keyword mentions
- 100-120 primary + LSI keywords combined
- Natural ratio: 80% primary, 20% LSI

**Example Integration**:
Instead of: "Use the zone 2 calculator for training zones."
Try: "Use the zone 2 calculator to determine your aerobic base building zones."
Better: "Use the zone 2 calculator to establish your fat burning zone and aerobic capacity foundation."
Best: "Use the zone 2 calculator to determine your maximum aerobic function zone, critical for aerobic base building and fat oxidation training."

## LSI Keywords NOT to Use

- Unrelated medical terms (cardiac, coronary, etc. - focus on training instead)
- Overly technical jargon without context
- Keywords from competing fitness verticals (weight loss, muscle gain, injury recovery)
- Brand names of specific apps/devices (use generic equivalents)
- Outdated terminology (e.g., "aerobics" instead of "aerobic training")
