Understanding Fitness Metrics

🫁

VO2 Max

Maximum oxygen uptake capacity - the gold standard for aerobic fitness measurement.

🔥

Lactate Threshold

The intensity at which lactate accumulation exceeds clearance - key for endurance performance.

📊

Performance Testing

Regular assessment provides objective data for training progression and goal setting.

Fitness Assessment Tips

📅 Regular Testing

Test every 6-8 weeks to track fitness improvements and adjust training zones.

🎯 Consistent Conditions

Use similar conditions (time, weather, course) for accurate comparison between tests.

📈 Progressive Goals

Set realistic improvement targets based on current fitness level and training history.

🔄 Multiple Metrics

Use various assessment tools to get a complete picture of your fitness profile.
