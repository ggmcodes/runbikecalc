Why Heart Rate Training?

🔬

Scientific Precision

Train at precise intensities based on your individual physiology for optimal adaptations.

📈

Track Progress

Monitor improvements in cardiovascular fitness and adjust training zones as you progress.

⚖️

Balanced Training

Ensure proper distribution between easy and hard training for sustainable improvement.

Heart Rate Training Tips

📱 Use a Monitor

Invest in a quality heart rate monitor for accurate real-time feedback.

🕐 Time in Zone

Focus on time spent in target zones rather than just average heart rate.

📅 Regular Testing

Retest your zones every 6-8 weeks as fitness improves.

🌡️ Consider Factors

Account for heat, stress, fatigue, and caffeine affecting heart rate.
