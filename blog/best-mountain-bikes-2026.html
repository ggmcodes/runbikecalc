Best XC Bike: Pivot Mach 4 SL Pro X0

The Fastest Cross-Country Race Bike Available

XC RACE WINNER

Pivot Mach 4 SL Pro X0

100mm DW-Link suspension with SRAM X0 Transmission. Incredibly efficient climbing with surprising descending capability.

$8,999 Check Price at Backcountry → (15% off first order)

The Pivot Mach 4 SL is the gold standard for cross-country racing. Pivot's DW-Link suspension provides exceptional pedaling efficiency—there's virtually no bob even when hammering out of the saddle. Yet unlike many XC race bikes that feel like hardtails on technical descents, the Mach 4 SL's 100mm of travel remains active and capable.

The SRAM X0 Transmission build provides wireless shifting reliability with the new T-Type cassette system. At around 23 lbs complete, it's light enough for racing while still feeling robust for aggressive riding.

Best Trail Bike: Santa Cruz Bronson CC X0

The Do-Everything Trail Bike

TRAIL CHAMPION

Santa Cruz Bronson CC X0

150mm VPP suspension, mixed wheel size (29/27.5), and proven reliability. The ultimate trail bike.

$8,299 Check Price at Backcountry → (15% off first order)

The Santa Cruz Bronson is the definitive trail bike. Its 150mm of VPP suspension delivers plush small-bump compliance while remaining supportive through compressions. The mixed wheel setup (29" front, 27.5" rear) combines 29er rollover with playful handling.

Santa Cruz's build quality is legendary—these frames survive years of abuse. The lower-link VPP design provides exceptional ground tracking while maintaining efficient climbing characteristics. It's the bike that can handle everything from after-work trails to bike park days.
