Best Aero Bike: Cervelo S5 Red eTap AXS

The Fastest Road Bike on the Market

AERO CHAMPION

Cervelo S5 Red eTap AXS

Wind-tunnel optimized aerodynamics, integrated cockpit, SRAM Red eTap AXS wireless shifting. The bike of WorldTour champions.

$9,750 Check Price at Backcountry → (15% off first order)

The Cervelo S5 represents the pinnacle of aero road bike design. Every tube shape has been optimized in Cervelo's wind tunnel to minimize drag at real-world yaw angles. The integrated cockpit eliminates cable-induced drag while maintaining a clean aesthetic.

Despite its aero focus, the S5 rides remarkably well. Cervelo's engineers balanced stiffness for power transfer with enough compliance to handle rough roads. The SRAM Red eTap AXS groupset provides flawless wireless shifting with the precision racers demand.

Best Climbing Bike: Cervelo R5 Frameset

The Featherweight Climbing Machine

CLIMBING CHAMPION

Cervelo R5 Road Frameset

Ultra-lightweight carbon construction optimized for climbing. Build with your choice of components.

$3,300 Check Price at Backcountry → (15% off first order)

The Cervelo R5 is the pure climber's choice. Where the S5 prioritizes aerodynamics, the R5 focuses entirely on low weight and responsive handling. The frameset tips the scales at just 780g, making it one of the lightest production frames available.

Available as a frameset, the R5 lets you build your dream climbing bike with your preferred components. Pair it with lightweight wheels and a Shimano Dura-Ace or SRAM Red groupset for a sub-15 lb climbing weapon.

Best Endurance Bike: Cervelo Caledonia 5 Frameset

All-Day Comfort Without Sacrificing Speed

ENDURANCE CHAMPION

Cervelo Caledonia 5 Frameset

Race DNA meets endurance geometry. More comfortable position with tire clearance up to 34mm.

$2,500 Check Price at Backcountry → (15% off first order)

The Cervelo Caledonia 5 brings race bike DNA to the endurance category. It's not a slow, plodding comfort bike—it's a fast road bike with a more sustainable position. The taller head tube and shorter reach let you ride all day without destroying your back.

Tire clearance up to 34mm means you can run wider tires for added comfort and grip on rough roads. The Caledonia 5 is perfect for gran fondos, long training rides, and riders who want performance without the aggressive position of race bikes.
