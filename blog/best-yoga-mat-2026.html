Frequently Asked Questions

What thickness yoga mat should I choose?

4-6mm is standard. 6mm+ for sensitive joints (knees, wrists). 3-4mm for balance work (feel the floor). Under 3mm (travel mats) for portability, use over carpet. Most yogis land on 5mm.

PVC vs natural rubber yoga mats?

PVC (Manduka PRO, Lululemon Reversible) — durable, easy to clean, long-lasting. Less eco-friendly. Natural rubber (Jade, Liforme) — better grip, more eco, latex allergy risk, smells like rubber initially, absorbs sweat.

How do I clean a yoga mat?

50/50 water + white vinegar in spray bottle. Spray, wipe with cloth, air dry. Manduka mats can tolerate harsher cleaners; Jade/natural rubber mats need gentler care. Avoid soaking — damages adhesives between layers.

How long should a yoga mat last?

Manduka PRO: 5-10+ years (lifetime guarantee). Lululemon Reversible: 3-5 years. Jade Harmony: 2-3 years. Budget mats: 6 months to 2 years. More use = less life. Investment mats pay off over time.
