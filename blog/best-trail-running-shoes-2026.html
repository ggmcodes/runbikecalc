Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best trail running shoe for 2026?

The Hoka Speedgoat 6 is the best all-around trail running shoe. It offers exceptional grip, cushioning, and protection for technical terrain. The Vibram Megagrip outsole handles rocks, roots, and mud with confidence.

Do I need trail running shoes or can I use road shoes?

Trail shoes provide critical advantages: aggressive tread for grip, rock plates for protection, and reinforced uppers for durability. On technical terrain, road shoes are dangerous‚Äîyou'll slip and potentially injure yourself.

What's the difference between Hoka Speedgoat and Salomon Speedcross?

Speedgoat has more cushioning and works on varied terrain. Speedcross has aggressive lugs for soft mud/loose terrain but less cushioning. Speedgoat is more versatile; Speedcross is specialized for muddy conditions.

How often should I replace trail running shoes?

Trail shoes typically last 300-500 miles depending on terrain. Replace when you see worn lugs, compressed midsole, or notice less grip. Technical trails wear shoes faster than smooth paths.
