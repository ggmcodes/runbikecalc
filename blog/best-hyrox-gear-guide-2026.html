Frequently Asked Questions

What shoes should I wear for Hyrox?

Nike Free Metcon 6 is the most popular for a reason — flat sole for sled work, enough cushioning for 8km running. Alternatives: Reebok Nano (stiffer, better for heavy stations), NoBull Runner+ (lighter). Avoid: pure running shoes (too soft for sled work) or pure Olympic lifters (too stiff for running).

Hyrox Open vs Pro vs Doubles?

Open — standard weights (barbell/sled/sandbag/wall ball), most athletes start here. Pro — heavier weights + weighted vest required. Doubles — team of 2 alternating stations, same weights as Open. Start with Open, progress to Pro after 2-3 events.

Do I need a weighted vest for Hyrox?

Only for Pro division (20% of athletes). Open division = no vest. For training, weighted vest adds progressive overload for running fitness + conditioning. If you're doing Pro, 15lb+ adjustable vest (Hyperwear Elite) is ideal.

What grip protection do I need?

Farmers carry, sled push, and sled pull destroy hands over a race. Picsil Azor or Bear KompleX palm grips prevent calluses + tears. Essential for multi-event competitors; optional for 1-event-per-year racers.
