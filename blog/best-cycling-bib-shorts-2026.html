Frequently Asked Questions

How should bib shorts fit?

Snug compression all over — legs, seat, core. Bib straps flat against shoulders (no bunching). Chamois sits flat against your contact points. Too tight restricts blood flow; too loose allows chamois to shift and cause chafing.

Do I wear underwear with bib shorts?

No — never. Bib shorts are designed to be worn directly against skin. The chamois has antimicrobial treatments and specific padding zones that underwear disrupts.

How long do bib shorts last?

1-3 years of regular use. Chamois wears out before the shell. Signs it's time: chamois flattens, bib straps lose elastic, leg grippers stop gripping. Wash in cold water, hang dry to extend life.

What makes a good chamois?

Multi-density foam (harder at sit bones, softer at surrounding tissue). Anti-bacterial treatment. Perforated breathability zones. Seamless construction at high-pressure points. The $250+ bibs have better chamois than $100 bibs — this is where quality scales.
