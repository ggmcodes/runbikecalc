Frequently Asked Questions

Does the 20-lb extra capacity on the Feedback matter?

If you own an e-bike now or plan to: absolutely. Most full-size adult e-bikes weigh 50-65 lbs. At 65-lb capacity, the Park Tool is right at its limit with an e-bike — no safety margin. The Feedback at 85 lbs has real headroom. If you only work on road/gravel/MTB: both work.

Can I really take the PRS-25 to races?

Yes, that is literally what it was designed for. It fits in a team van, folds in 90 seconds, and deploys in 90 seconds. Mechanics at pro races mount it to the back of the van for in-race fixes.

Feedback lifetime vs Park Tool 2-year — is Feedback actually better warranty?

Feedback Sports honors lifetime warranties on clamps, legs, and hardware for original owner. Park Tool 2-year is generous for a mechanical product but worse on paper. In practice both brands have excellent customer service; most failures occur in year 1.

Which is easier to use daily?

Park Tool PRS-25 cam-action clamp opens/closes slightly faster with one hand. Feedback Sports 360° clamp is more versatile for awkward wheel/frame positions. For a shop doing 10 bikes/day: Feedback wins on flexibility. For 1-2 bikes/day at home: PRS-25 is enough.
