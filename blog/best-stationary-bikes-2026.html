Frequently Asked Questions

Is Peloton worth the money in 2026?

Peloton is worth it if you value instructor motivation and community. The Bike+ ($2,495) offers the best experience, but you'll pay $50/month for membership. For pure hardware, alternatives like Schwinn IC4 offer comparable ride quality at half the price.

What is the best Peloton alternative?

The Schwinn IC4 ($999) is the best Peloton alternative. It connects to Peloton's app via Bluetooth, has 100 resistance levels, and saves you $1,500+ upfront plus lower monthly costs.

Can you use Peloton app with other bikes?

Yes. The Peloton app ($12.99/month) works with any stationary bike. Bikes with Bluetooth (Schwinn IC4, Bowflex VeloCore) can sync cadence to the app. You won't get automatic resistance, but classes work great.

Peloton Bike vs Bike+: What's the difference?

The Bike+ adds: 24" rotating screen (vs 22" fixed), auto-follow resistance, Apple GymKit, and better speakers. The original Bike is $1,000 less and delivers 90% of the experience.
