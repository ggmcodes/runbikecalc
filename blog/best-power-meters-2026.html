Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best power meter for 2026?

The Favero Assioma Duo is the best power meter for most cyclists. At $749, it offers dual-sided power measurement, excellent accuracy (Â±1%), easy installation, and simple battery swaps.

Pedal vs crank vs spider power meter: which is best?

Pedal power meters are easiest to install and swap between bikes. Crank-based are more aerodynamic. Spider-based are most accurate but expensive. For most cyclists, pedal-based offers the best combination of convenience and value.

Do I need dual-sided power?

Single-sided doubles your left leg's power to estimate total. This works for most training. Dual-sided measures both legs independentlyâ€”useful for identifying imbalances. The price difference has shrunk, making dual worth it.

Are power meters worth the money?

Yes, if you train seriously. Power enables precise zones, race pacing, fitness tracking, and structured workouts. Heart rate lags and is affected by many factors. Power is instant and objective.
