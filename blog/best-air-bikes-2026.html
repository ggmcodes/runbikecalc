What is the best air bike for 2026?

Assault Bike vs Rogue Echo: which is better?

Is the Concept2 BikeErg an air bike?

Yes, the BikeErg uses air resistance but without moving arm handles‚Äîlegs only. It's ideal for cycling-specific training and Zwift compatibility, but not for full-body conditioning like traditional air bikes.

How loud are air bikes?

Air bikes produce 70-85 decibels during intense efforts‚Äîsimilar to a vacuum cleaner. Belt-drive bikes (Echo, BikeErg) are quieter than chain-drive (Assault). Not ideal for apartments with noise-sensitive neighbors.
