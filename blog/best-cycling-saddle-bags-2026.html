Frequently Asked Questions

How big of a saddle bag do I need?

Road commutes: 0.3-0.5L (tube, multi-tool, tire levers, CO2). All-day rides: 1-1.5L (add pump, phone, snacks). Bikepacking: 6-15L. Size by longest planned ride; too small = carry backpack on big days.

Strap vs rail-clip mounts?

Strap — compatible with any saddle, stable if tightened properly, takes longer to remove. Rail-clip (QuickClick) — fast release, but only works with specific saddles. For one-bike setups, rail-clip wins on convenience.

Water-resistant vs waterproof?

Water-resistant (standard saddle bags) — fine for light rain, keeps drizzle off your tube. Waterproof (Ortlieb) — fully sealed, ride through downpours without soaking contents. For PNW/UK/mountain riders, waterproof matters.

Should I take my saddle bag off between rides?

If your bike is garaged, leave it on. If you park outside at work/cafes, remove it — bag + tools can be quickly stolen. Ortlieb-style QR mounts make daily removal fast.
