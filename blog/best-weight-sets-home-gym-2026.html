Why Endurance Athletes Need Strength Training

Research-Backed Benefits

  • • Running economy: 2-8% improvement with strength training
  • • Cycling economy: 3-5% improvement in power output
  • • Injury prevention: Stronger muscles protect joints and connective tissue
  • • Sprint capacity: Better kicks and surges
  • • Bone health: Critical for long-term athlete health

Strength Training for Endurance Athletes: Key Exercises

For Runners

  • • Single-leg squats
  • • Romanian deadlifts
  • • Hip thrusts
  • • Step-ups
  • • Nordic curls

For Cyclists

  • • Back squats
  • • Leg press
  • • Single-leg deadlifts
  • • Core work (planks, pallof press)
  • • Upper body rows

Frequency: 2-3 sessions per week, 20-40 minutes each. Focus on compound movements, 2-4 sets of 6-12 reps.
