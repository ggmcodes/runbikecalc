Frequently Asked Questions

Are these training plans really free?
Yes, all training plans are completely free. The .ics calendar export is free with no signup required. PDF and spreadsheet exports require a free email signup.
What training methods are used?
Our plans incorporate proven principles from coaches like Jack Daniels, Joe Friel, and the 80/20 Endurance methodology. Plans include periodized Base, Build, Peak, and Taper phases with appropriate recovery weeks.
How do I add the plan to my calendar?
After generating your plan, click the "Calendar (.ics)" button to download the file. Import it into Google Calendar, Apple Calendar, Outlook, or any calendar app that supports .ics files.
Who is RunBikeCalc for?
RunBikeCalc is for all endurance athletes - from beginners training for their first 5K to experienced athletes preparing for Ironman or ultramarathons.
