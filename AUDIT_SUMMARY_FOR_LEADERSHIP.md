# RunBikeCalc Content Audit: Executive Summary

## Key Findings

RunBikeCalc has a **strong foundation** with 200+ blog articles and 45+ calculators covering running, cycling, and triathlon. However, significant **content gaps exist** in specialized niches with proven high search volume that competitors are exploiting.

## Quick Stats

- **Current Content:** 200+ blog articles, 45+ calculators
- **Coverage:** Well-developed in marathon, general cycling, heart rate zones
- **Major Gaps:** Testing protocols, women athletes, MTB, injury recovery, environmental training
- **Opportunity Size:** 25+ significant gaps with 5K-20K+ monthly searches each
- **Estimated Total Addressable Opportunity:** 200K-300K additional monthly searches

## Risks & Mitigations

### Content Quality Risk
- **Mitigation:** Internal review + external expert review for specialized topics
- **Especially important for:** Women's cycle content, overtraining assessment, injury recovery

### Medical/Legal Risk
- **Mitigation:** Clear disclaimers, peer-reviewed citations, professional consultation
- **Especially for:** Return-to-running, women's health, overtraining assessment

### Competitor Response
- **Mitigation:** Unique angles, comprehensive clusters, multi-sport focus
- **Build:** Defensible topical authority through interconnected content

### Traffic Cannibalization
- **Mitigation:** Hub-based architecture prevents direct competition
- **Example:** New FTP protocol article links to existing FTP calculator, doesn't replace it
