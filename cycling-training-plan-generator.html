How This Cycling Training Plan Works

Our cycling training plan generator creates personalized, periodized plans based on Joe Friel's proven methodology and modern training science.

Workout Types

  • Endurance Rides (RPE 3-4): Build aerobic base at conversational pace
  • Sweet Spot (RPE 5-6): Maximum efficiency training just below threshold
  • Tempo Rides (RPE 6-7): Improve sustainable power output
  • Climbing Repeats (RPE 7-8): Build strength for hilly events
  • VO2 Intervals (RPE 8-9): Increase aerobic ceiling
