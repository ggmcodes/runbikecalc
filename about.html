About RunBikeCalc

Our Mission

RunBikeCalc provides free, accurate, and easy-to-use calculator tools for runners, cyclists, and endurance athletes. Our mission is to support your training goals with scientifically-based calculations that help you train smarter and perform better.

About the Creator

RunBikeCalc was created by Glen Gomez, an avid cyclist and endurance athlete who completed the Ironman 70.3 in 2025. As someone deeply passionate about data-driven training and performance optimization, Glen recognized the need for accessible, accurate calculation tools that could enhance every athlete's training experience.

"I built these tools because I needed them in my own training," says Glen. "Whether I was calculating my Zone 2 heart rate for base building, determining my FTP for power-based cycling workouts, or planning race pacing strategies, I found myself constantly searching for reliable calculators. Instead of settling for scattered, inconsistent tools, I decided to create a comprehensive platform that brings together all the calculations an endurance athlete needs."

Every calculator on RunBikeCalc has been personally tested and refined through Glen's own training journey. The result is a collection of tools that are not only scientifically accurate but also practical and user-friendly for athletes at every level, from weekend warriors to competitive racers.
