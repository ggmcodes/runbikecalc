Frequently Asked Questions

Are these training plans really free?

Yes! Calendar (.ics) export is completely free with no signup required. PDF and spreadsheet exports require a free email signup to help us keep the service running.

What training methods are used?

Our plans incorporate principles from Jack Daniels, Joe Friel, 80/20 Endurance, and proven ultramarathon coaching methodologies. Each plan uses periodization with Base, Build, Peak, and Taper phases.

Can I customize the training plans?

You can set your experience level, weekly availability, and preferred long workout day. Export to spreadsheet format for full customization of individual workouts.

How do I add the plan to my calendar?

Download the .ics file and import it into Google Calendar, Apple Calendar, or Outlook. Each workout will appear as a calendar event with full details including workout type, duration, and instructions.
